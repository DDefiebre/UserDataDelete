Delete Your Anwalt Guru Account & Data

If you wish to delete your Anwalt Guru account and associated data, please follow the steps outlined below.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to daniel.defiebre@gurusense.de with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your email used in the app.

Types of Data Deleted

Upon account deletion, all stored messages, payment history, and user information will be permanently removed.
